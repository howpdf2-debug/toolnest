Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

ToolNest is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our free online tools.

Files & Data Processing

All file processing on ToolNest happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to our servers. We cannot see, access, or store any files you process using our tools.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal anonymous usage data through Google Analytics (page views, browser type, general location) to improve our services. We do not collect personal information, file contents, or document data.

Cookies

We use cookies for: dark mode preference storage (local), and Google AdSense advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Advertising

ToolNest uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

Third-Party Services

We use: Google Analytics (usage statistics), Google AdSense (advertising), and CDN libraries (PDF.js, pdf-lib, etc. for tool functionality). These services have their own privacy policies.
