Quick Summary: ToolNest is committed to protecting your privacy. Most of our tools process files directly in your browser, meaning your files never leave your device. We only collect minimal data necessary to operate and improve our service.

When you visit ToolNest, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Browser type and version

  • Time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses

1.2 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when:

  • Contacting us through our contact form

Important: Most of our tools process files directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. This means:

  • Your files are NOT uploaded to our servers
  • Your files NEVER leave your device
  • We do NOT store, access, or process your files
  • All processing happens locally on your computer

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

3.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. We use cookies to improve your experience on our site.

3.2 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

3.3 Managing Cookies

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of this website may become inaccessible or not function properly.
