About ToolNest

Free online tools for everyone, everywhere

Our Mission

ToolNest believes powerful tools shouldn't come with a price tag. We provide high-quality, free online tools that make everyday tasks easier for students, professionals, freelancers, and businesses across India and the world.

What We Offer

20+ professional tools including PDF merge, compress, split, rotate, convert, image compression, QR code generator, GST invoice generator, password generator, word counter, JSON formatter, and SEO meta tags generator.

Why 100% Free?

We're supported by non-intrusive Google AdSense advertisements. We'll never charge for features, add watermarks, or put tools behind paywalls. Your trust is more valuable than any subscription fee.

Privacy First

All tools process files locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device — we cannot see, store, or access your documents. Zero server uploads.

Made for India

ToolNest was built keeping Indian users in mind — GST invoice generator, UPI QR codes, and tools that work well even on slower connections.

Our Story

Started in 2026 by BhaiHelp.in, ToolNest began with a few PDF tools and grew based on user feedback. Today we serve thousands of users daily from students to businesses.
