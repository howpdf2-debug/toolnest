ATS Kya Hai aur Kyun Important Hai?

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) ek software hai jo HR ke liye resumes automatically filter karta hai. Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Amazon India — sab large companies ATS use karti hain.

ATS Kaise Kaam Karta Hai:

  • Resume mein job description ke keywords dhundhta hai
  • Keywords match hone par resume HR tak pahunchata hai
  • Keywords nahi mile → resume reject, HR ne dekha bhi nahi

ATS-Friendly Resume Ke Rules:

  • Simple formatting: Tables, columns, text boxes avoid karo — ATS parse nahi kar sakta
  • Standard fonts: Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman — fancy fonts avoid karo
  • Keywords use karo: Job description se exact keywords resume mein daalo
  • PDF ya DOC format: ATS dono accept karta hai
  • No images/graphics: Photo, logo, fancy charts — ATS ignore karta hai
💡
Keyword tip: Job description padhein carefully — "Python developer" likha hai toh resume mein "Python" clearly mention karo. "Proficient in coding" nahi — specific naam daalo.

Resume Format — Kaunsa Best Hai India Ke Liye?

1. Chronological (Most Common — 5+ years experience):

Experience reverse chronological order mein — latest job pehle.

2. Functional (Career Change ke liye):

Skills ko highlight karta hai — career gap hai toh use karo.

3. Combination/Hybrid (Best for Freshers aur 2-5 years):

Skills + Experience dono — India mein most effective format.

Page Length:

  • Freshers (0-2 years): 1 page strictly
  • Mid-level (3-7 years): 1-2 pages
  • Senior (8+ years): 2-3 pages maximum

Section-by-Section Resume Guide

1. Contact Information (Top):

  • Full Name (bold, large)
  • Phone number (active)
  • Professional email — firstname.lastname@gmail.com (cute nicknames avoid karo)
  • LinkedIn URL (customize: linkedin.com/in/yourname)
  • City, State (full address nahi chahiye)

2. Professional Summary (3-4 lines):

HR pehle yahi padhta hai. Include: job title, years of experience, key skills, value proposition.

Example: "Results-driven Java Developer with 4 years experience building scalable applications. Expertise in Spring Boot, Microservices, AWS. Delivered 3 projects serving 500K+ users."

3. Skills Section:

  • Specific tools, languages, frameworks daalo
  • Categorize: Languages | Frameworks | Databases | Tools
  • Only real skills — jo actually aate hain

4. Work Experience:

  • Company name, designation, dates (Month Year)
  • 3-5 bullet points har role ke liye
  • Achievements numbers mein: "Reduced API response time by 40%", "Increased sales by Rs 12L"
  • Action verbs: Led, Developed, Implemented, Achieved

5. Education:

  • Degree, University/College, Year, CGPA/Percentage
  • Freshers ke liye education top par rakho

Common Resume Mistakes — Avoid Karein

  • ❌ Generic objective: "Seeking a challenging position to grow" — specific professional summary likho
  • ❌ Responsibilities list: "Responsible for coding" — achievements batao, responsibilities nahi
  • ❌ Spelling mistakes: Grammarly use karo — ek spelling mistake bhi careless impression deta hai
  • ❌ Generic email: cooldude123@gmail.com — firstname.lastname@gmail.com use karo
  • ❌ Overdesigning: Fancy colors, multiple columns — ATS nahi padhta, HR distract hota hai
💡
Proofreading tip: Resume finish karne ke baad ek raat chhod do. Kal fresh eyes se padhna — mistakes dikhenge. Phir kisi trusted person se bhi review karwao.
