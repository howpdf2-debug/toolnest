Common Student PDF Problems Solved

Problem 1: "File too large to email"

Solution: Compress images before adding to documents, or split large PDFs into smaller parts.

Problem 2: "Need to print specific pages only"

Solution: Convert PDF to images, select needed pages, then merge back to PDF.

Problem 3: "Multiple PDFs for one assignment"

Solution: Use PDF merger to combine all parts into single file.

Problem 4: "Can't read PDF on phone"

Solution: Convert to images for easier mobile viewing and annotation.
