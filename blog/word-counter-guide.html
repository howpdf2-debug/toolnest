In this guide: Everything you need to know about word counting — character limits for social media, ideal blog post length, essay requirements, and SEO content guidelines.

Why Word Count Matters

Every platform has limits. Twitter caps at 280 characters. Instagram allows 2,200. LinkedIn optimal is 1,300-2,000 words. Google ranks long-form content (1,500+ words) higher for informational queries.

Platform Limits Quick Reference

  • Tweet: 280 characters
  • Instagram caption: 2,200 characters
  • LinkedIn post: 3,000 characters
  • Meta description: 155-160 characters
  • Title tag: 60 characters
  • WhatsApp message: 65,536 characters

Blog Post Length Guide

  • 1,500-2,500 words: Standard informational post
  • 2,500-4,000 words: Comprehensive guide
  • 4,000+ words: Pillar content, high-ranking potential
  • Under 1,000 words: News/updates only

Academic Word Counts

  • Essay: 500-5,000 words depending on level
  • Research paper: 3,000-8,000 words
  • Thesis/Dissertation: 10,000-100,000 words
  • Abstract: 150-300 words
