Legal Ways to Save YouTube Videos

  • ✅ YouTube Premium: Official offline download feature
  • ✅ Creator Permission: Ask video owner for download rights
  • ✅ Creative Commons: Videos with CC licenses can be downloaded
  • ✅ Your Own Content: Download your own uploaded videos
  • ✅ Screen Recording: For personal, non-commercial fair use only

YouTube's Terms of Service

According to YouTube's Terms of Service, downloading videos without permission from YouTube or the content creator is prohibited unless a download button or link is clearly provided by YouTube. Always support creators by watching ads, subscribing, and sharing their content legally.

Responsible Usage
